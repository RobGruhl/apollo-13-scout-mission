‚Ä¢ Sights on specific known stars

=== METHOD #3: INERTIAL MEASUREMENT UNIT (IMU) ===

‚Ä¢ Gyroscope-based platform
‚Ä¢ "Remembers" spacecraft orientation
‚Ä¢ Updated by star sightings or manual alignment

‚Ä¢ Acts as stable platform in space
‚Ä¢ Like inner ear for balance on Earth

The Problem:
‚Ä¢ Gyros drift over time (loses accuracy)

‚Ä¢ Normally use star sightings
‚Ä¢ Apollo 13 used Sun/Earth instead

‚Ä¢ "Tells" IMU which way is which
